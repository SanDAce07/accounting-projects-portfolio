# Reconciliation Notes

## Likely Reconciling Items In This Sample
- **Outstanding check**: a book-side check recorded but not yet shown as cleared on the bank statement
- **Deposit in transit**: a late deposit recorded in books but not yet shown by the bank
- **Bank service charge**: appears on the bank statement and must be recorded in books
- **Interest income**: appears on the bank statement and must be recorded in books
- **NSF item**: customer payment reversed by the bank and requires follow-up

## Suggested Portfolio Talking Points
- Compared book records to bank activity and documented timing differences and unmatched items.
- Identified bank-only adjustments and explained how they affect the adjusted cash balance.
- Built the project to reflect month-end close and reconciliation support responsibilities common in staff accounting roles.
