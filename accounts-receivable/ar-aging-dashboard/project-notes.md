# Project Notes

## Recommended Metrics
- Total open balance
- Total overdue balance
- Percentage of AR that is overdue
- Top 5 customers by overdue balance
- Oldest invoice age
- Balance by aging bucket

## Useful Excel Formulas
- `=TODAY()-due_date_cell` for days past due
- nested `IF` statements or `IFS` for aging buckets
- `SUMIFS` for customer and bucket summaries
- `XLOOKUP` for customer-level reference mapping

## Suggested Portfolio Talking Points
- Built a dashboard to classify receivables by age and identify overdue concentration.
- Used Excel formulas and pivot tables to summarize invoice balances and support collections review.
- Designed the project to mirror real AR review workflows used in month-end close and cash flow monitoring.
